## The Golden Rules

### Rule 1: Invest at Least 20% of Take-Home Salary

The 50-30-20 rule is a good starting point:
- 50% for needs (rent, food, utilities)
- 30% for wants (entertainment, dining out)
- **20% for savings/investments**

### Rule 2: Start with What's Comfortable, Then Increase

If 20% feels too much right now, start lower:
- **Minimum:** 10% of salary
- **Ideal:** 20% of salary
- **Aggressive:** 30%+ of salary

The key is to start and increase with salary hikes.
