## Goal Tracking

### Annual Review Checklist

| Check | Action |
|-------|--------|
| Progress vs target | On track? Need adjustment? |
| Goal cost change | Re-estimate with inflation |
| Life changes | Marriage, child, job change |
| Risk alignment | De-risk as approaching |

### When to Adjust

| Scenario | Action |
|----------|--------|
| Behind target | Increase SIP or extend timeline |
| Ahead of target | Reduce risk, maintain SIP |
| Goal postponed | Reassess strategy |
| Goal cancelled | Redirect to other goals |
