No pension from employer? Don't worry. You can create your own monthly pension using Systematic Withdrawal Plan (SWP) from mutual funds.

SWP lets you withdraw a fixed amount monthly from your investments, giving you regular income while your remaining corpus continues growing.

## What is SWP?

A Systematic Withdrawal Plan automatically redeems a fixed amount from your mutual fund at regular intervals (usually monthly).

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Frequency | Monthly, quarterly, or as needed |
| Amount | Fixed sum you choose |
| Source | Your mutual fund investment |
| Taxation | Only gains portion taxed |

## Tax Efficiency of SWP

### How SWP is Taxed

Each SWP redemption has two components:
1. **Principal (cost):** Not taxed
2. **Gains:** Taxed based on holding period and fund type

| Fund Type | Short-term (< 1 year) | Long-term (> 1 year) |
|-----------|----------------------|---------------------|
| Equity/Hybrid (65%+ equity) | 20% | 12.5% above â‚¹1.25 L/year |
| Debt/Conservative | Slab rate | Slab rate |

### Why SWP is More Tax-Efficient Than FD

**â‚¹6 L annual income need, 30% tax bracket:**

| Source | Gross Needed | Tax | In Hand |
|--------|--------------|-----|---------|
| FD Interest | â‚¹8.57 L | â‚¹2.57 L | â‚¹6 L |
| SWP (equity fund) | â‚¹6.3 L | â‚¹0.3 L* | â‚¹6 L |

*Only gains portion taxed, and at 12.5% LTCG (after â‚¹1.25 L annual exemption).

**SWP can save significant taxes** depending on your gains ratio and tax bracket.

### Tax Optimization Strategies

| Strategy | How It Works |
|----------|--------------|
| FIFO redemption | First-in-first-out means older (long-term) units sold first |
| Multi-fund SWP | Spread across funds to stay under â‚¹1.25 L LTCG exemption |
| Equity fund preference | 12.5% LTCG vs 30% slab rate on debt |

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

### 1. Starting with Too High a Rate

| Starting Rate | Risk |
|---------------|------|
| 3-4% | Safe |
| 5-6% | Risky for 25+ year horizon |
| 7%+ | Likely corpus depletion |

**Solution:** Start conservative, increase if corpus grows.

### 2. Using Only Equity Funds

| Scenario | Risk |
|----------|------|
| Market crashes 40% | Your corpus is â‚¹60 L instead of â‚¹1 Cr |
| You continue â‚¹40K SWP | Now 8% withdrawal rate |

**Solution:** Use balanced or hybrid funds; maintain cash buffer.

### 3. Not Reviewing Annually

| Check | Why |
|-------|-----|
| Corpus value | Is it growing or shrinking? |
| Withdrawal rate | Has it crept up? |
| Fund performance | Is it meeting expectations? |

### 4. Single Fund Dependency

| Risk | Solution |
|------|----------|
| Fund underperforms | Diversify across 2-3 funds |
| AMC issues | Use different fund houses |
